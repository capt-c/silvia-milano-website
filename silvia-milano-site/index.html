Research focus

What I work on

⚖️

Ethics of Recommender Systems

How algorithmic recommendations shape information access, autonomy, and the social fabric — and what ethical frameworks can evaluate and reform them.

🔍

Epistemology & AI

Epistemic dimensions of algorithmic profiling, fairness, and AI-mediated knowledge, including hermeneutical injustice and epistemic polarisation.

🎲

Self-Locating Belief

Bayesian approaches to de se uncertainty — beliefs about who you are, where you are, and when you are — and their implications for rational choice.
