Research

Research

I work at the intersection of formal epistemology, ethics of AI, and philosophy of technology, with a particular focus on how algorithmic systems shape knowledge, values, and rational agency. See also my Google Scholar profile.

Research themes

Algorithmic Recommendation

What recommender systems do to knowledge, values, and autonomy — and what ethical and regulatory frameworks can address the harms they cause.

AI & Epistemic Justice

How AI systems mediate who gets to know what — epistemic polarisation, hermeneutical injustice, and algorithmic profiling as sites of epistemic harm.

Formal Epistemology

Bayesian approaches to de se belief and rational updating under uncertainty — and what these frameworks reveal about agency in AI-mediated environments.
